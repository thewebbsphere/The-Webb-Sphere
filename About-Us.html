Fast Load Time
Speed is Key

A website that takes more than 3 seconds to load is considered slow, slowly lose clients, therefore, we ensure that images are optimized, codes are minified and Content Delivery Networks are in place.

We use tools like Google PageSpeed Insights, GTmetrix and Pingdom to test the speed of a website and make sure it is fast enough to load in less than 3 seconds.

Accessible and Usable
User Experience from different perspectives.

A website that is accessible to all users, including those with disabilities, is a must. We ensure that the website is usable by all users, regardless of their browsers, devices or assistive technologies.

The website should be easy to navigate, with clear and concise information, and should be designed to be user-friendly. We implement best practices for web accessibility, including using semantic HTML, providing alternative text for images, and ensuring that the website is keyboard-navigable.

S.E.O Optimized
Search Engine Optimization

A website that is not optimized for search engines will not be found by users, therefore, we ensure that the website is optimized for search engines, including using relevant keywords, meta tags, and alt text for images.

We also ensure that the website is structured in a way that is easy for search engines to crawl and index, including using a sitemap and robots.txt file. We use tools like Google Search Console and SEMrush to test the SEO of a website and make sure it is optimized for search engines.

Our Process

PLANNING
  • RESEARCH

    • We begin by understanding your business goals, target audience, and market trends. This foundational research ensures that our strategy aligns with your vision.

  • Strategy

    • Based on our research, we develop a comprehensive strategy that outlines the project scope, objectives, and key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure success.

  • Wireframes

    • We create wireframes to visualize the layout and structure of your website. This blueprint serves as a guide for the design and development phases, ensuring a user-friendly experience.

  • Content

    • Our team collaborates with you to gather and create compelling content that resonates with your audience, enhancing engagement and driving conversions.

DESIGN
  • Visual Design

    • Our designers craft a visually appealing aesthetic that reflects your brand identity. We focus on color schemes, typography, and imagery to create a cohesive look.

  • Mockups

    • We develop detailed mockups that showcase the website's design elements and layout. This step allows for feedback and adjustments before moving to the next phase.

  • Prototyping

    • Interactive prototypes are created to simulate user interactions. This helps us identify usability issues and refine the user experience before development begins.

  • User Interface

    • We design intuitive user interfaces that facilitate seamless navigation and interaction, ensuring that visitors can easily find what they need.

DEVELOPMENT
  • Front-end

    • Our developers bring the design to life by coding the front-end of the website. This includes creating responsive layouts and ensuring compatibility across devices and browsers.

  • Back-end

    • We build the back-end infrastructure that powers your website, including server-side logic, APIs, and integrations with third-party services to enhance functionality.

  • Database

    • Our team sets up a robust database to securely store and manage your data. This ensures efficient data retrieval and management for a smooth user experience.

DEPLOYMENT
  • Testing

    • Before launch, we conduct thorough testing to identify and fix any bugs or issues. This includes functionality, usability, and performance testing to ensure a flawless experience.

  • Launch

    • Once testing is complete, we launch your website, making it live for your audience. Our team monitors the launch to address any immediate concerns.

  • Maintenance

    • Post-launch, we provide ongoing maintenance and support to keep your website running smoothly. This includes regular updates, security checks, and performance optimization.
